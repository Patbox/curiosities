- Added Slime Boots:
  - Crafted with 4 Slime Balls and Leather Boots.
  - When falling from 1.5+ blocks, entity wearing them will bounce like on a Slime Block.
  - Fall damage will be converted to item damage.
  - It's max durability is 65 and provides one point of defense.
- Added Mining Helmet:
  - Crafted with 2 Copper Ingots, a Torch, a Glass Block and a Copper Helmet.
  - When worn, entity will emit light level of 10.
  - Has same defense and durability as Copper Helmet.
- Added Minecart with Jukebox:
  - Crafted with a Minecart and a Jukebox.
  - Works just like regular Jukebox, but it moves on rails!
- Fixed blocks being invisible through block target selection.
